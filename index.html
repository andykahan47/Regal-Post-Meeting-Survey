Customer meeting report

Complete after each customer visit or sample request.

Connect to Google Sheets — one-time setup

1

Create a new Google Sheet. In row 1, add these headers exactly:
Timestamp | Rep Name | Date | Customer | Contacts | Meeting Type | Sentiment | Patterns Liked | Patterns Disliked | Gaps/Requests | Business Health | Current Projects | Future Projects | Samples Left | Follow-Up Actions | Additional Notes

2

Go to Extensions → Apps Script in your Google Sheet. Paste the script below, then click Deploy → New deployment → Web app. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. Copy the Web App URL.
function doPost(e){var s=SpreadsheetApp.getActiveSpreadsheet().getActiveSheet();var d=JSON.parse(e.postData.contents);s.appendRow([d.timestamp,d.rep,d.date,d.customer,d.contacts,d.meetingType,d.sentiment,d.liked,d.disliked,d.gaps,d.bizHealth,d.currentProjects,d.futureProjects,d.samples,d.followUp,d.notes]);return ContentService.createTextOutput("ok");}

3

Paste your Web App URL below and click Save. Then share this HTML file with your sales reps.

✓ URL saved! Reps can now submit directly to your sheet.
